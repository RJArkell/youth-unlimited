Description: As the Jane & Finch Associate Director, Tope’s role is to assist in leading, supporting and developing staff, volunteers and interns to inspire the Jane & Finch neighbourhood by sharing God’s love. Tope sees the value in investing in children & youth through mentorship and community engagement. Her experience working with children & youth has ranged from residential care, camp work, before & after school programs, autism services, and serving children & youth through mentorship and community outreaches.
