Description: Ann is YU’s Counselling & Community Consultant: Youth Mental Health & Community Resources. Ann serves the full YU team — researching, resourcing, guiding and supporting our frontlines workers and ministries in a context in which youth and families increasingly are challenged with mental health and family issues. She has worked with “at-risk” families and in marginalized communities for close to 30 years and is also a therapist. She also sits on the executive of Young Parents of No Fixed Address (YPNFA) which is a city-wide network of agencies serving young families in the GTA.
