Description: As the Director of Engagement, Rachel's role is to strategically lead Youth Unlimited’s connection with donors, partners, and potential staff and volunteers. Through team leadership, communication strategies, and relationship-building, Rachel supports the sustainable growth and impact of Youth Unlimited’s ministries throughout Toronto. Rachel comes to Youth Unlimited with more than 10 years of experience in the non-profit and Christian ministry sectors, specializing in strategic communications management. Rachel has a passionate heart to see the transformation of young people in the Greater Toronto Area, and is thrilled to be a part of the YU team, using her expertise in communications to serve YU’s staff and Toronto’s youth each day.
