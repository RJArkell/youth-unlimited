Description:

Since joining the Youth Unlimited team in 2017, Jennifer has been excited to be part of contributing to the vision of transforming the lives of youth within the GTA.  She brings her prior professional experience as a Social Worker and volunteer work in family ministry to her role at YU.

As the Assistant to the Ministry Director, Jennifer coordinates scheduling and logistics, supports team leaders and field staff in reporting and metrics, compiles and analyzes ministry reports, and assists with special projects and the development of new ministry partnerships.  She loves that her role also involves supporting YU's ministry leaders and staff in both practical and intangible ways!
