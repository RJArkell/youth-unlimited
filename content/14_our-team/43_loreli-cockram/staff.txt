Description:

Loreli is passionate about drawing out God-given abilities and gifts in others, building community, and discovering and communicating stories of hope.
 
In her role as Young Parents Director, Loreli applies each of these passions to develop a ministry that welcomes and supports pregnant and parenting youth (ages 15-25) and their children. 
 
Loreli sees the potential in every young parent we serve. It is her privilege to accompany them on the journey to becoming a transformative presence in their homes and communities.
